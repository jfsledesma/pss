Who we are

The Philippine Snake Society was founded by a pair of snake enthusiasts who wanted to provide fellow snake lovers with a community to discuss, learn, and share the facts, myths, and importance of snakes in the Philippines. Since the establishment of our Facebook page in 2012, we've grown to a humble following of over 7,000 members and have collaborated with outlets such as Reuters, the Philippine Star, and GMA-7 in spreading awareness about our advocacy.

What we do

Although we ourselves are not certified researchers or scientists, our communications team keeps up to date with the literature concerning snakes, ranging from pet care to legislation to their role in environment. We believe in fostering respect between people and snakes, and educating people about proper snake handling, care, and the importance of maintaining their population.
